English Section
Respect. Integrity. Responsibility. Excellence.

Welcome to Pakistan International School Jeddah - English Section (PISJES), is The premier destination for quality education in Saudi Arabia. We are dedicated to fostering academic excellence and holistic development of our students. Our school offers a rich curriculum aligned with international standards enabling our students to receive a world-class education right here in Jeddah. Explore our diverse educational programmes, from primary to secondary education (K-12), designed to cultivate critical thinking, creativity, problem solving and leadership skills. Our experienced faculty and state-of-the-art facilities provide a nurturing environment where students can thrive academically and personally. At PISJES, we pride ourselves on our transparent admissions process, comprehensive curriculum, and diverse extracurricular activities designed to enhance our students "overall development".
